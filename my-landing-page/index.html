Tier 2: Ops & Automation Sprint

$5,000 to $7,000
one-time (30 days)

Maximum 3 sprint clients per month

A focused one-month build to fix a single critical operational problem. You pick the priority, I design and build the system that solves it.

Examples

  • RevOps cleanup (CRM audit, data hygiene, pipeline rebuild)
  • Airtable system build (client tracking, project management, reporting)
  • Automated onboarding workflows (client or employee)
  • Dashboard and reporting layer (connect your data, visualize what matters)
  • AI-assisted workflow setup (automate repetitive tasks)
  • SOP library and process documentation

What you get

  • Kickoff call and operational audit
  • Full system design and build
  • Final Loom walkthrough and documentation
  • One live handoff call

What happens after 30 days

  • The system is yours. I walk away.
  • No ongoing support unless you book Tier 1 or Tier 3
  • Revisions during the sprint are included; post-sprint changes are not
What most sprint clients discover: One system solves one problem, but it usually reveals three more. If you want ongoing support to maintain what we built (or tackle the next priority), most sprint clients move to Tier 3 within 60 days.
Best for: Teams with one clear operational bottleneck and the ability to maintain the system after I hand it off, or teams who want to test working together before committing to a retainer.
