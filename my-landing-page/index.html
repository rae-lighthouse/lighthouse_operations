one-time (30 days)

Maximum 3 sprints per month

A focused one-month build to fix one critical operational problem. You pick the priority, I design and build the system that solves it.

Examples

  • Client onboarding workflow redesign - Map the journey, build templates, automate handoffs
  • Revenue operations cleanup - CRM audit, pipeline rebuild, forecasting dashboards
  • Claims workflow automation - Form-to-database, assignment routing, approval tracking
  • KPI dashboard and reporting system - Connect data sources, visualize metrics that matter
  • Customer health tracking and retention dashboard - Usage metrics, risk scoring, renewal forecasting
  • SOP library and process documentation - Document core workflows, create training materials

What you get

  • Kickoff call and operational audit
  • Full system design and build

What happens after 30 days

The system is yours. I walk away. No ongoing support unless you book Advisory or Fractional. Revisions during the sprint are included; post-sprint changes are not.

What most sprint clients discover: One system solves one problem - but it usually reveals three more. If you want ongoing support to maintain what we built (or tackle the next priority), most sprint clients move to Fractional within 60 days.

Sprint-to-retainer offer: Move to Fractional within 30 days of your sprint completion and get your first month at $12,000 (normally $15k–$18k).
Perfect for: Teams with one clear operational bottleneck and the ability to maintain the system after I hand it off, or teams who want to test working together before committing to a retainer.

Who I Am

I'm Rae Schneider and I've cut onboarding times in half, maintained 100% client retention for 2+ years, and built operational systems that scaled healthtech companies from chaos to clarity.

At Stork Club (fertility benefits platform), I was Head of Revenue & Customer Operations and built the operational backbone that allowed us to scale enterprise clients:

  • Cut client onboarding time from 75 to 40 days by mapping the full implementation journey, creating project management templates, consolidating materials, and automating handoffs
  • Maintained 100% client retention and 92 NPS for 2+ years while managing enterprise accounts with complex needs
  • Built automated claims workflow that moved surrogacy and adoption claims from manual spreadsheets to a system using Jotform and Airtable - complete with auto-routing, staff notifications, and approval tracking. This workflow became the foundation for the product roadmap.
  • Created real-time KPI dashboards in Tableau tracking the entire revenue cycle from authorization through payor payment - giving leadership visibility into forecasting, customer health, and operational bottlenecks
  • Managed 300+ authorizations and 150+ payor claims per month across 11 enterprise clients, tracking everything from submission to payment
  • Led operational discussions around claims processing as part of insurance integration launches (though I don't lead the technical payor integration work itself)

Before healthtech, I built operational systems across industries:

  • Implemented ERP systems for retail operations scaling from $1M to $10M ARR
  • Designed and launched national programs at Mitacs serving 10,000+ participants
  • Built PowerApps automation that cut manual workload by 25% across an 80-person team

I specialize in diagnosing what's broken, building systems that fix it, and documenting everything so it doesn't break again.
