Ops & Automation Sprint

Maximum 3 sprints per month

A focused one-month build to fix one critical operational problem. You pick the priority, I design and build the system that solves it.

Examples

  • AI tool implementation: select and configure the right tools, integrate with existing workflows, build automations around them
  • Client onboarding workflow redesign: map the journey, build templates, automate handoffs
  • Revenue operations cleanup: CRM audit, pipeline rebuild, forecasting dashboards
  • Process workflow automation: form-to-database, assignment routing, approval tracking
  • KPI dashboard and reporting system: connect data sources, visualize metrics that matter
  • Customer health tracking: usage metrics, risk scoring, renewal forecasting
  • SOP library and process documentation: document core workflows, create training materials

What you get

  • Kickoff call and operational audit
  • Full system design and build
  • Final Loom walkthrough and documentation
  • One live handoff call

What happens after 30 days

The system is yours. I walk away. No ongoing support unless you book Advisory or Fractional. Revisions during the sprint are included; post-sprint changes are not.

What most sprint clients discover: One system solves one problem, but it usually reveals three more. Most sprint clients move to Fractional within 60 days.

Sprint-to-Retainer Offer: Convert to Fractional within 30 days of sprint completion for a discounted first month.
Perfect for Teams ready to act on a clear priority — whether surfaced through an AI Assessment or already known. One focused build, fully handed off. Most sprint clients discover more to fix and move to Fractional within 60 days.

Fractional Operations Lead

Limited to 2 clients

Part-time operational support for teams that need someone who can both think and build. I handle workflow chaos, improve systems, and keep things moving without the overhead of a full-time hire.

What this covers: Operational execution, not executive strategy. I fix systems and workflows; you handle P&L, board, and hiring decisions.

What you get

  • Weekly working sessions (1–2 per week)
  • Hands-on implementation of systems and automations
  • Project and workflow cleanup
  • Reporting dashboards and operational visibility
  • Light team coordination
  • Async support between calls

Scope

  • 15–20 hours per week max
  • Focus areas determined monthly; we prioritize together

Not included

  • P&L ownership or financial management
  • Executive leadership or board-level strategy
  • Hiring, firing, or HR functions
  • Fundraising support
  • Technical integration or engineering work
Perfect for Series A and growth-stage companies with traction but no operational muscle. Often the right move after an AI Assessment reveals more opportunities than a single sprint can cover — you need someone embedded who can work through the full roadmap.
